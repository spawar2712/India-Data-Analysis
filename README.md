Project Title: India Data Analysis 

Project Description:

This project focuses on analyzing India’s sales performance data using Microsoft Excel to gain valuable business insights. The interactive dashboard visualizes key performance indicators such as 
Total Sales (₹589,650), 
Total Profit (₹129,071),
 Total Quantity (5,758),
 Profit Margin (22%), and 
Total Shipping Cost (₹61,780).

The dashboard includes multiple visual components such as:

Sales and Profit by Sub-category: Compares profitability across different product types.
Monthly Sales Trend: Displays sales fluctuations and growth patterns over the year.
Sales and Profit by Category: Identifies which product categories contribute the most to overall revenue.
Profit by Shipping Mode: Analyzes profitability based on shipping methods (First Class, Same Day, etc.).
Sales by Segment and Region: Shows business performance across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments in various regions.
Profit Margin Distribution (Donut Chart): Visualizes profit share across categories.

Sales Before and After Six Months: Tracks sales performance improvement over time.
Tools Used:
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Charts (Bar, Line, Donut)
Slicers and Filters
Conditional Formatting

Key Insights:
Technology and Office Supplies generated the highest revenue.
Profitability is highest in the Corporate segment.
Standard Class shipping mode contributes most to profits.
Sales improved significantly after the mid-year mark.
